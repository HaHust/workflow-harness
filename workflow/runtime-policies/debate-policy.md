# Debate Policy

Debate is a bounded W01-managed procedure, not a custom agent.

Triggers:
- Worker and reviewer disagree after one repair.
- Multiple architecture options have meaningful tradeoffs.
- Security, performance, migration, release, or compatibility risk remains unresolved.
- F01 cannot classify root cause with enough confidence.
- M01 and M02 disagree about workflow evolution.

Each round records claim, evidence, counterargument, revision, and W01/R02 verdict. Max rounds: 3.
